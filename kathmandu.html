Explore Kathmandu

Kathmandu, the capital city of Nepal, is rich in history, culture, and spirituality. It is known for its ancient temples, bustling markets, and vibrant culture.

Things to Do in Kathmandu

  • Visit the iconic Swayambhunath Stupa (Monkey Temple)
  • Explore the historic Durbar Square
  • Take a spiritual journey to Pashupatinath Temple
  • Stroll through the streets of Thamel for shopping and dining
  • Experience the local culture and festivals
