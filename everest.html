Explore Mount Everest Base Camp

Mount Everest Base Camp (EBC) is one of the most iconic trekking destinations in the world, offering an unparalleled adventure through the heart of the Himalayas. The trek provides trekkers with the opportunity to stand at the base of the world’s highest peak, Mount Everest (8,848m).

Things to Do at Mount Everest Base Camp

  • Trek through the stunning Khumbu Valley, passing by traditional Sherpa villages
  • Visit the Tengboche Monastery, one of the most significant monasteries in the region
  • Experience the breathtaking views of Everest, Lhotse, and Nuptse
  • Explore the vibrant Namche Bazaar, the gateway to Everest
  • Capture the unforgettable sunrise over Everest from Kala Patthar
