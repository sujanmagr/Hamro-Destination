Explore Bhaktapur

Bhaktapur, also known as Bhadgaon, is a historic city in the Kathmandu Valley known for its well-preserved medieval architecture. Bhaktapur Durbar Square, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is the heart of the city, showcasing beautiful temples, palaces, and statues.

Things to Do in Bhaktapur

  • Visit Bhaktapur Durbar Square to admire the Vatsala Temple, Nyatapola Temple, and the 55-Window Palace
  • Explore the narrow streets filled with traditional pottery and handicraft shops
  • Experience the unique festivals and cultural events, such as the Bisket Jatra
  • Enjoy traditional Newari cuisine at local eateries
  • Visit the Dattatreya Square and the Peacock Window, a masterpiece of woodcarving
