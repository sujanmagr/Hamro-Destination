Explore Lumbini

Lumbini, the birthplace of Lord Buddha, is one of the holiest places for Buddhists around the world. Located in the Rupandehi District of Nepal, Lumbini is a UNESCO World Heritage Site that attracts pilgrims and tourists who come to visit its ancient temples and monasteries.

Things to Do in Lumbini

  • Visit the Maya Devi Temple, marking the exact birthplace of Buddha
  • Explore the Sacred Garden with its many monasteries built by Buddhist communities from around the world
  • See the Ashoka Pillar, erected by Emperor Ashoka in 249 BC
  • Attend meditation and prayer sessions in the peaceful surroundings
  • Learn about Buddhist history and teachings at the Lumbini Museum
