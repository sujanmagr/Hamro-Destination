Explore Pokhara

Pokhara is a picturesque city known for its serene lakes, stunning mountain views, and adventurous activities. It's a gateway to the Annapurna Circuit and offers a tranquil escape for travelers.

Things to Do in Pokhara

  • Boating on Phewa Lake and visiting the Tal Barahi Temple
  • Paragliding with panoramic views of the Himalayas
  • Exploring the World Peace Pagoda
  • Visiting Davis Falls and Gupteshwor Mahadev Cave
  • Trekking to Sarangkot for sunrise views
