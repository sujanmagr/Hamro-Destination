Explore Chitwan

Chitwan National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is one of the most famous wildlife-viewing parks in Asia. Located in the subtropical lowlands of Nepal, Chitwan is home to a diverse range of flora and fauna, including the endangered one-horned rhinoceros, Bengal tiger, and Gharial crocodile.

Things to Do in Chitwan

  • Go on a jungle safari to spot wildlife, including rhinos and tigers
  • Enjoy a canoe ride on the Rapti River to see crocodiles and birds
  • Visit the Elephant Breeding Center
  • Experience a cultural dance show by the Tharu community
  • Take a guided nature walk to learn about the park's ecosystem
