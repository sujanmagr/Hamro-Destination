Explore Janakpur

Janakpur is a city of great religious and historical significance, known as the birthplace of Sita and the setting for her marriage to Lord Ram. The city is famous for the Janaki Mandir, a grand temple dedicated to Sita, attracting pilgrims from all over.

Things to Do in Janakpur

  • Visit the Janaki Mandir, an iconic temple with intricate architecture
  • Explore the numerous ponds and gardens that dot the city
  • Participate in the vibrant festivals, especially Ram Navami
  • Learn about the city's history and culture at the Ram Sita Vivah Mandap
  • Shop for traditional Mithila art and handicrafts
