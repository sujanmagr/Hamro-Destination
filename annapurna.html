Explore Annapurna Base Camp

Annapurna Base Camp (ABC) is one of the most popular trekking destinations in Nepal, offering breathtaking views of the Annapurna massif, including Annapurna I (8,091m), the 10th highest mountain in the world. The trek takes you through diverse landscapes, from lush forests to high-altitude terrain.

Things to Do at Annapurna Base Camp

  • Trek through picturesque villages and terraced fields
  • Enjoy panoramic views of the Annapurna and Dhaulagiri ranges
  • Experience the unique culture of the Gurung and Magar communities
  • Relax in natural hot springs at Jhinu Danda
  • Witness stunning sunrises and sunsets over the mountains
